FAQ ?

Here are some of the Roomsy frequently asked questions.

  How do I register my child?

You can register your child by creating an account as a parent/guardian, then filling out the child registration form under your dashboard.

 Is my child's information secure?

Yes, we use secure encryption protocols to keep all personal and medical information safe and private.

 Can I contact the childcare provider directly?

Yes, once a booking is confirmed, you’ll have access to secure messaging with the childcare provider through your dashboard.

 What type of childcare services are available?

We support daycare centers, babysitters, preschool programs, and after-school care options.

 Do you provide real-time updates?

Yes, providers can send real-time updates about your child’s activities, meals, naps, and more throughout the day.
