Daycare Services

Safe and Nurturing Environment

Our daycare program offers a safe, clean, and engaging environment for children aged 6 months to 5 years. We provide age-appropriate learning activities, meals, and nap times under professional supervision.

We follow a well-balanced daily schedule that includes story time, music and movement, arts and crafts, outdoor play, and quiet rest periods. Our team of experienced caregivers ensures that every child receives individual attention and care, while also learning how to socialize and build friendships with their peers.
